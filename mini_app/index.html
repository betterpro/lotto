LottoChee group lottery pools for Canadian players

LottoChee helps groups across Canada pool into official lottery tickets for Lotto Max, Lotto 6/49 and Daily Grand. Members buy shares from $3, see every ticket before the draw and split any prize by share.

How it works

  1. Buy a share in an open group round.
  2. The group trustee buys the official lottery tickets.
  3. Tickets are photographed and attached before the draw.
  4. Any winnings are split by share and credited to members.

Transparency and responsible play

Every round has ticket proof, written share records and clear prize-split rules. LottoChee is for players 19+ located in Canada and is not affiliated with, endorsed by or operated by any official lottery operator.
